What is Hexabones?

Hexabones wants to be a solid foundation to start a brand new e-commerce design. It's lovely handcrafted to work side by side with PrestaShop. Our goal is to provide an efficient tool for designers & developpers who are starting from scratch to design quality and user centered templates.

Want to be part?

Hexabones is an open-source project, you can participate to its conception by discussing major decisions about UX, SEO, marketing, and more on the community boards or pushing some great lines of code in the git repo.

What it is not?

  • Started
  • A theme for production
  • An official PrestaShop's project
  • A skater's brand ;)

Fast, efficient and easy to use, PrestaShop's free e-commerce solution provides everything you need to open, operate and maintain a successful online store. PrestaShop.com
